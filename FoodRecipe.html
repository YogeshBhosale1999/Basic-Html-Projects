Matar Paneer Recipe :Matar paneer is a restaurant style creamy,delicious and rich peas paneer curry.
Matar paneer is one of the India's most popular paneer dishes.In most indian homes,it is made during
winters when fresh peas are available.However,it can be made throughout the year by readymade frozen
peas. It tastes amazing to servewith rice,naan or paratha.The matar paneer is best when you plan for
celebration!!I like this vegetable because it tastes good as well as it is nutricious to health.

Ingredients to make Matar paneer :-

    =>To fry and puree:

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 cardamoms or elaichi
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger or adrak(cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup onions chopped(1 large or 2 medium)
  • 1 & 1/2 cup tomatoes(2 large chopped)
  • 10 cashewnuts
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    =>For matar paneer gravy :

  • 2 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 inch cinnamon or dalchini(optional)
  • 1 small bay leaf(optional)
  • 3/4 or 1 tsp red chilli powder(less spicy variety)
  • 1/8 tsp turmeric or haldi
  • 3/4 or 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 or 3/4 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 or 3/4 cup green peas or matar(boiled or frozen)
  • 1 or 1 & 1/4 cup water(use more if needed)
  • 1 green chilli deseeded and slit(optional)
  • 250 grams paneer(indian cottage cheese)or tofu
  • 1 tsp dried fenugreek leaves (Kasuri methi)
  • 1/2 tsp salt(as needed)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves or cilantro
  • 3 or 4 tbsp cream

Recipe to make the Matar paneer :-

    =>Preparation of matar paneer gravy :

  1. If you are using the store bought paneer,soak it in a bowl of hot water for 20 mins.
  2. Drain the water and now paneer is ready to use.This is usually done for softening the paneer.
  3. Pour 1 tbsp oil into a hot pan.
  4. When the oil becomes hot,add 2 green cardamoms,ginger and garlic.
  5. Fry for a minute.
  6. Next add 1 cup chopped onions and fry until they turn light golden in color.
  7. Put 1 & 1/2 cup tomatoes,1/8 tsp turmeric and 1/4 tsp salt.
  8. Saute for 2 or 3 minutes.
  9. Cook it until it becomes soft and creamy.
  10. Add 10 cashewnuts and let it cool completely.
  11. Make fine puree of this mixture in a blender.Don't add water to it.

    =>Preparing the matar paneer :

  1. Heat 2 tbsps oil in the same pan.
  2. Saute 1 inch cinnamon & 1 bay leaf for a minute.
  3. Pour the pureed onion tomato mixture.
